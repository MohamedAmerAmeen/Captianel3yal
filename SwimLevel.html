Swim Levels

Tadpoles (Level-1)

ADJUST TO THE WATER: 1 MONTH TO 12 MONTHS WITH PARENT (2 CHILDREN MAX, PARENT-ASSISTED CLASS)

  • Create confidence for caregivers when handling their baby in the water.
  • Equip caregivers with knowledge for proper holds and safe submersions.
  • Learn initial skills to breath holding.
  • Introduce back float.
  • Establish fundamentals of rolling over into the back.
  • Learn how to reach and turn to the pool's edge.

Minnows (Level-2)

UNDERSTAND SAFE BOUNDARIES AROUND WATER: 13 MONTHS TO 30 MONTHS WITH PARENT (2 CHILDREN MAX, PARENT-ASSISTED CLASS)

  • Learn breath control by submersion.
  • Learn how to safely return to the pool's edge.
  • Establish independent kicking movement, back floats and rollovers.

Leapfrogs (Level-3)

BECOME INDEPENDENTLY COMFORTABLE IN THE WATER: 2.5 YEARS & UP (2 CHILDREN MAX).

  • Independent breath control by safe submersions.
  • How to roll over and back float while moving.
  • How to safely get to the pool's edge and climb out.
  • How to float on their stomach for proper body positioning as they swim.

Seahorse (Level-4)

GAIN CONTROL WHILE SWIMMING: BASED ON ABILITY (2 CHILDREN MAX).

  • Jump into the water and swim freestyle.
  • Begin implementing arm and leg coordination.
  • Float on their stomach while kicking with control.
  • Master breath control with independent safe submersions.

Starfish (Level-5)

DEVELOP INTO AN INDEPENDENT SWIMMER: BASED ON ABILITY (2 CHILDREN MAX)

  • How to use their judgement and experience from level 1-4 determine what they can and cannot accomplish by themselves in the water.
  • How to breath on their side.
  • Streamlining body position.
  • The freestyle stroke with side breathing.
  • Breaststroke breathing, arms & kicks.

Seals (Level-6)

DEVELOP INTO AN INDEPENDENT SWIMMER: BASED ON ABILITY (2 CHILDREN MAX)

  • Strength & tempo of the freestyle stroke, backstroke and breaststroke.
  • Treading water without assistance.

Sharks (Level-7)

DEVELOP SWIM STROKE TECHNIQUE: ADVANCED SWIMMERS (2 CHILDREN MAX)

  • Freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke and sidestroke technique.
  • Fundamental components of the butterfly and flip turns.

Stingrays (Level-8)

MASTER SWIM STROKE PROFICIENCY: ADVANCED SWIMMERS (2 CHILDREN MAX)

  • Properly perform all the strokes taught in the 8 levels if the Captain El3yal swim program with correct technique.
  • Perform flip turns and open wall turns.
